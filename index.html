Learn How to Write a Sentence by Cathlyn Lopio

Writing a sentence is easy when you know the basic rules of grammar. Follow these simple steps to write a sentence correctly:

Step 1: Start with a Capital Letter

Every sentence should start with a capital letter. For example:

She is going to the store.

Step 2: Use Correct Punctuation

Sentences should end with either a period (.), exclamation mark (!), or question mark (?). For example:

What time is it?

Step 3: Make Sure Your Sentence Makes Sense

Your sentence should convey a complete thought. For example:

The cat sat on the mat.
